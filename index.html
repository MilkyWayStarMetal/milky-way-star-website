Company Overview

Milky Way Star provides recycling, recovery, and refining services for metal-bearing industrial residues and secondary raw materials.

We work with manufacturers, trading companies, recyclers, and industrial partners worldwide to recover value from zinc, copper, nickel, vanadium spent catalyst, brass, copper concentrate, zinc ore, and other recyclable metal by-products.

Our services cover material sourcing, inspection, quality classification, warehouse handling, logistics coordination, and downstream processing partnerships. We help customers reduce waste, improve resource utilization, and return valuable metal resources to the global supply chain.

We provide practical metal recycling and recovery solutions for customers handling metal-bearing residues, recyclable by-products, and secondary raw materials.

Our service flow is designed for reliable international cooperation: transportation, collection, sorting and processing, and recovered material utilization.
